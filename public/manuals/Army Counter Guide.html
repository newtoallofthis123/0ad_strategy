CORE The Counter Triangle MEMORIZE THIS

Every army boils down to three roles. Each beats one, loses to one.

Mental shortcut: Spears stop horses. Horses run down archers. Archers shred spears. That's the whole game.

REF.03 When is Cavalry Useful? FOUR JOBS

Cavalry is not your main combat unit. It's a tool. Use it for specific jobs.

// JOB 01 — SCOUTING

Find their base, expansions, idle armies. Always have 1-2 horses on patrol. Replace instantly if killed.

// JOB 02 — RAID WORKERS

Fastest unit, picks off villagers far from base. The single biggest source of "why am I losing" if you don't do this.

// JOB 03 — SNIPE SIEGE

Their ram rolls up. Your cav charges from behind. Siege has zero melee defense. Free kill.

// JOB 04 — FLANK RANGED

Ride around the pike wall and crash into the archer line behind it. Kills the DPS, the pikes become harmless.

NEVER: charge cav into pikes. You will lose 80% of them in 5 seconds.
SCENARIOS I See X — What Do I Do? EMERGENCY PROCEDURES
// SEE: Cavalry approaching base
  1. Garrison workers immediately — houses, towers, CC. One hotkey saves your eco.
  2. Train spearmen / pikemen NOW. Even 5-6 spears in a clump scares cav off.
  3. Pull them under a tower. Towers + spears = cav graveyard.
  4. Don't chase. Cav is faster. They'll kite back and re-raid elsewhere.
// SEE: Wave of archers / skirmishers
  1. Send cavalry around the flank — never engage ranged from the front, they kite.
  2. No cav? Charge with swordsmen through them; ranged melts in melee.
  3. Don't fight with spearmen head-on — archers eat spears alive.
  4. Towers help. Ranged has to stop to shoot, towers out-DPS them.
// SEE: Pikeman block / phalanx pushing in
  1. Don't melee them. Pikes hit first and hard.
  2. Slingers + skirmishers at range — pikes are slow, kite them while you shoot.
  3. Swordsmen or elephants for a head-on break if you must.
  4. Look for siege behind the pikes — that's the real threat. Pikes are bodyguard.
// SEE: Siege (rams, helepolis, towers)
  1. Cavalry charge — NOW. Siege has no melee defense. Your cav deletes it.
  2. If already at wall, sally out a small squad from a gate. Don't let it work.
  3. Garrison the wall section being attacked — archers inside towers shoot back.
  4. After siege dies, retreat. Don't get drawn into the full engagement.
// SEE: War elephants lumbering at you
  1. Pikemen, lots of them. Pikes have huge bonus damage vs elephants.
  2. Mass ranged from distance — slingers especially.
  3. Don't bunch up. Elephants do splash/trample damage. Spread out.
  4. Towers do bonus damage to elephants. Use them.
// SEE: Mixed army (the realistic case)

Real armies are combined arms: pikes front, archers back, cav on wings, siege in rear. You can't counter all of it. Pick a role to win:

  • Kill the siege first if your walls are at stake
  • Flank the archers with cav — kills the DPS, pikes become harmless
  • Retreat into kill zone (towers + chokes) and attrition them
Fundamental defense move: let them attack into your towers + walls + garrisoned ranged. You win the trade just by existing there.
ENDPLATE Rules to Remember MEMORIZE
  • Spears stop horses. Pikes stop them harder.
  • Cavalry kills anything that can't kill it back fast.
  • Ranged beats melee — unless melee gets in their face.
  • Siege dies to anything but walls.
  • Garrisoning workers is free defense. Use it every time.
  • Towers triple the value of whatever's inside them.
  • The army that fights on its own terrain wins.
  • Real armies are mixed. Pick a role to win, not the whole fight.
